Spatial Drivers of Fire Intensity

We don’t know much about what drives fire intensity across different scales. To address this, fire radiative power data were obtained from FIRMS for the period 1 Jan 2004 to 1 Jan 2014.

Data were subset to only include fire detections of > 95% confidence.

Fire detections were then associated with * Mean annual precipitation * Geologic parent material * Woody cover
